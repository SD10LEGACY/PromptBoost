# Privacy Policy for PromptBoost

**Effective Date:** April 2026

PromptBoost ("the Extension") is built on the principle of privacy by design. This policy outlines how your data is handled when you use the Extension. 

### 1. Data Collection
**PromptBoost does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.** We do not use telemetry, tracking scripts, or third-party analytics services. 

### 2. API Keys
Any API keys provided by the user (e.g., Gemini, Groq, Mistral, OpenRouter) are stored **locally** on your device using the browser's secure `chrome.storage.local` API. 
* Your keys are never transmitted to the developer or any centralized database.
* Your keys are used strictly to authenticate requests directly from your browser to your chosen AI providers.

### 3. Prompt Content
Your text and prompts are processed in real-time. **PromptBoost does not log, save, or harvest your prompts.** Your keystrokes and generated content remain entirely on your local machine and the respective AI platform you are interacting with.

### 4. Transparency
PromptBoost is fully open-source. Anyone can inspect the code to verify these privacy claims. You can review the complete source code in our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/SD10LEGACY/PromptBoost).

### 5. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy or data, please open an issue on our [GitHub Issues page](https://github.com/SD10LEGACY/PromptBoost/issues).
